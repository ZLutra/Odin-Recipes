Spinach & Artichoke dip

Discription

A spinach dip recipe passed onto me from my older brother. Start with a squeeze of fresh lemon in a bowl no more than 1.5 teaspoons

Ingrediants

  • 2 tubs of light cream cheese (355g)
    • \
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 cup grated parmeasan cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated italiano shredded cheese blend
  • 1/2 cup smoked aged & medium shredded cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of freash chopped/minced garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of garlic puree (bottle)
    • 1 teaspoon dired basil 1 teaspoon parsley 1/2 teaspoon garlic salt 1/2 teaspoon himalayan salt 1/2 teaspoon black pepper 1/3 cup smoked aged & medium shredded cheese or cheese of choice

Intructions

  1. mix together ingrediants in a bowl

    2. after mixed add 1 full jar of artichoke hearts drained and chopped

  2. Lightly mix / whip finished chopped / minced artichaoke into spinach mixture

  3. bread 3-4 packages of garlic naan bread

  4. cut bread into pita triangles and lightly coat with virgin olive oil

  5. toast bread on aluminum sheet or baking sheet until desired golden brown texture
